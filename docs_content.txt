Opt-In Language Reference
Prepared: March 2026
Copy these exactly into your UI. Placement matters — consent language must appear adjacent to the phone number input field, not buried in a footer or linked document only.
How to Implement
The consent language must be:
Visible without scrolling — adjacent to the phone number field
In a readable font size — no smaller than 12px
Not pre-checked — user must actively provide their phone number (that IS the opt-in)
Present at every point where phone numbers are collected
Recorded in your database — store consent timestamp and source (booking form, portal signup, etc.)
TIPPD — Booking Form (Public Website)
Place directly below the phone number input field on the booking wizard step that collects phone number.
Primary Consent Language
"By providing your phone number, you agree to receive automated SMS text messages from [Business Name] about your dumpster rental service, including booking confirmations, delivery windows, driver updates, and invoices. Reply STOP to opt out at any time. Reply HELP for help. Message and data rates may apply. View our Privacy Policy at [URL] and Terms of Service at [URL]."
Short Version (for tight UI)
"By providing your number, you consent to receive service-related SMS from [Business Name]. Reply STOP to opt out. Msg rates may apply. Privacy Policy | Terms"
Checkbox Version (optional additional confirmation)
☐ I agree to receive text message updates about my dumpster rental service from [Business Name]. Reply STOP to opt out at any time.
TIPPD — Contractor Portal Signup
Place on the account creation form adjacent to the phone number field.
"By providing your phone number, you agree to receive SMS messages from [Business Name] and Tippd related to your contractor account, including booking confirmations, scheduling updates, invoices, and payment reminders. Reply STOP to opt out. Message and data rates may apply."
TIPPD — Driver Onboarding
Place on the driver account setup screen.
"Your phone number will be used to send you job notifications, route updates, and operational alerts via SMS. Reply STOP to opt out. Standard message and data rates may apply."
RŌM — Account Creation (Guest)
Place directly below the phone number field on the signup form.
Primary Consent Language
"By providing your phone number, you agree to receive SMS messages from Rōm (romlife.co) about your account and bookings, including confirmations, reminders, messages from guides, and payment notifications. Reply STOP to opt out at any time. Reply HELP for help. Message and data rates may apply. View our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service."
Short Version
"By providing your number, you consent to receive booking-related SMS from Rōm. Reply STOP to opt out. Msg rates may apply. Privacy Policy | Terms"
RŌM — Guide Account Creation
Place on the guide onboarding form adjacent to the phone number field.
"By providing your phone number, you agree to receive SMS messages from Rōm about your guide account, including new booking notifications, guest messages, payout confirmations, and platform updates. Reply STOP to opt out at any time. Message and data rates may apply."
STOP / HELP Auto-Responses
Configure these in Twilio — they fire automatically when a user texts STOP or HELP.
Tippd STOP Response
"You have been unsubscribed from [Business Name] SMS notifications. You will no longer receive text messages. For service inquiries call or email us at [contact info]."
Tippd HELP Response
"[Business Name] help: For service questions call [phone] or email [email]. To unsubscribe reply STOP. Tippd support: [support email]."
Rōm STOP Response
"You have been unsubscribed from Rōm SMS notifications. You will no longer receive text messages. Manage your account at romlife.co or email support@romlife.co."
Rōm HELP Response
"Rōm help: support@romlife.co | romlife.co. Reply STOP to unsubscribe from all SMS."
Database Fields Required
Add these to both platforms' customer/user tables to record consent:
Tippd — customers table additions
sms_consent: boolean default false
sms_consent_at: timestamptz nullable
sms_consent_source: text nullable — booking_form | portal_signup | driver_onboarding
sms_opted_out_at: timestamptz nullable — set when STOP received
Rōm — profiles table additions
sms_consent: boolean default false
sms_consent_at: timestamptz nullable
sms_consent_source: text nullable — guest_signup | guide_onboarding
sms_opted_out_at: timestamptz nullable
